About MEPIE Foundation

Empowering communities through education, training, and development—one person at a time

Our Mission

At the MEPIE Foundation, we believe that compassion is a practical tool for building stronger communities.

Our mission is to demonstrate a way of life rooted in helping rather than competing—supporting individuals through education, training, and development so they can uplift others in turn.

Our work is designed to create a ripple effect: empower one person, and they become capable of empowering many more.

Our Charitable Objects

Primary Purpose

To advance education, skills development, and technical training for the public benefit, particularly in underserved and developing communities, including but not limited to Cameroon.

How We Achieve This:

  • Establishing and supporting technical and vocational education initiatives
  • Providing access to practical, technology-focused learning environments
  • Supporting infrastructure, equipment, and training resources
  • Promoting self-sufficiency, employability, and sustainable economic development

Our First Project

The Charity's first project is the ME Technical School in Yaounde, Cameroon, focused on practical technical education and skills development.

Our Legal Structure

Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO)

MEPIE Foundation is registered as a Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) with the Charity Commission for England and Wales.

A CIO is a legal structure specifically designed for charities, offering:

  • Limited liability protection for trustees
  • Direct registration with the Charity Commission
  • Simpler governance than company structures
  • Legal entity status (can own property, employ staff, enter contracts)

Foundation Model

We operate as a CIO Foundation Model, which means:

  • Trustees have full control and responsibility
  • No separate membership body
  • Streamlined decision-making
  • Trustees are accountable to the Charity Commission and the public

This model suits our mission perfectly—allowing us to move quickly, respond to needs, and maintain complete transparency.
